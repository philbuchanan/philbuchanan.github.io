Phil Buchanan

Designer, Developer and Instructor

Phil Buchanan is the Head of Design at Hubdoc in Toronto. He is also a Course Director and Instructor in the Department of Design at York University, teaching Fundamentals and Methods: Visual Interaction Design and Processes and Workflows: Visual Interaction Design to first and second year students.

Prior to working at Hubdoc he was the Manager of Product Design at EventMobi. He graduated with honours from the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design.
