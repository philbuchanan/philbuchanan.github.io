Phil Buchanan

Designer, Developer and Instructor

Phil Buchanan is a design leader in Toronto, interested in growing people, building design systems, and rapid prototyping. He is a Course Director and Instructor in the Department of Design at York University, teaching Fundamentals and Methods: Visual Interaction Design and Processes and Workflows: Visual Interaction Design to first and second year students.

Previously, Phil was the Head of Design at Hubdoc and the Manager of Product Design at EventMobi. He graduated with honours from the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design.
