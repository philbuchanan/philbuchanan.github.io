Phil Buchanan

Designer, Developer and Instructor

Phil Buchanan is the Lead Product Designer at EventMobi in Toronto. He is also an instructor in the Department of Design at York University, teaching Fundamentals and Methods: Visual Interaction Design and Processes and Workflows: Visual Interaction Design to first and second year students.

Prior to working at EventMobi he was a Digital Art Director at Context Creative. He graduated with honours from the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design.
