Phil Buchanan is a product leader, designer, and educator in Toronto.

Introduction

Phil is a product leader with over 15 years of experience applying design thinking to create innovative, easy to use, and delightful products that embrace the advances and complexities of technologies.

Phil is the Director of Product and Engineering at Tetra Trust—the first regulated digital asset custodian in Canada—where he is responsible for product, design, and engineering. Previously, Phil ran product and design teams at several startups, and developed and delivered interaction design curriculum to hundreds of students in the Department of Design at York University.

He graduated with honours from the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design.
