Phil Buchanan is a product design leader and educator in Toronto.

Phil is the Director of Digital at Aubs & Mugg Inc., responsible for digital strategy, design, and development. Previously, Phil was the Head of Design at Hubdoc, managing the product and marketing design teams.

Phil is deeply invested in the growth and development of himself, his peers, and future generations of designers. For six years he was a course director in the Department of Design at York University, developing and delivering interaction design curriculum to first and second year students.

He graduated with honours from the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design.
