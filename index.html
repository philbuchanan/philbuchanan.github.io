Phil Buchanan is a product leader, designer, and educator in Toronto.

Phil is a product leader with over 15 years of experience applying design thinking to create innovative, easy to use, and delightful products that embrace the advances and complexities of technologies.

Phil is the Director of Digital at Aubs & Mugg Inc., responsible for product, design, and development. Previously, Phil was the Head of Design at Hubdoc (acquired by Xero in 2018), managing the product and marketing design teams, and a course director and lecturer in the Department of Design at York University, developing and delivering interaction design curriculum to first and second year students.

He graduated with honours from the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design.
