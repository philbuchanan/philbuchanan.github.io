Phil Buchanan—Designer, Developer and Instructor

Phil Buchanan is a Toronto based product designer at EventMobi. He is also an instructor in the Department of Design at York University, teaching Interactivity Design to first and second year students. Prior to working at EventMobi he was a digital art director at Context Creative. In 2008 he graduated with honours from the York University/Sheridan College Joint Program in Design.
