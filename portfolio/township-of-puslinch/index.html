Township of Puslinch

Product, Design, Development • Web, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue.js

The Township of Puslinch needed to deliver a large and diverse set of information with a complex hierarchy in a simple, easy to navigate website.

Features included:

  • Multiple levels of navigation allowing constituents to further refine their navigation based on topic areas
  • Prominent and robust fuzzy search functionality with the ability to highlight search terms in content
  • A document library that provided quick access to important forms and documents that would otherwise be buried deep in content
  • A custom council calendar with the option to subscribe to upcoming events as well as meeting video posting notifications
